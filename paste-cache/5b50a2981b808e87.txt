## Implementation Guide

### When to Use Each Template Type

| Template Type | Frequency | Best Time |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Weekly Check-In | Weekly | Monday morning or Friday afternoon |
| Problem-Solving | Bi-weekly | When you notice common challenges |
| Content Share | As needed | When you find genuinely valuable content |
| Debate/Hot Take | Monthly | When you want to boost engagement |
| Resource Compilation | Monthly | Start of month (gives time to compile) |
| Behind-the-Scenes | Monthly | Last week of month |
| Introduction | Pinned | Always available |
| Lesson Learned | Encourage weekly | After members try something new |
| Resource Review | Encourage bi-weekly | After members use new tools |
| Case Study | Encourage monthly | After members achieve wins |
| AMA | Monthly | Spotlight different members |
| Challenge | Quarterly | Start of season/quarter |

### Customization Tips

**For Each Template:**

1. Adjust tone to match your brand (formal vs. casual)
2. Add relevant emoji for your niche (ğŸ¨ for creatives, ğŸ’» for tech, etc.)
3. Include community-specific terminology or inside jokes
4. Modify structure to fit your community's preference (longer vs. shorter)
5. Add relevant hashtags if you use them for organization

### Success Metrics

**Track These for Each Template:**

- Number of responses/comments
- Time to first response
- Quality of responses (subjective but important)
- Whether the post achieved its intended purpose
- Member feedback on template usefulness

**Iterate Based On:**

- Response rates (if low, template might be too complex or unclear)
- Quality of contributions (if low-effort, prompt needs to be more specific)
- Member feedback (ask what would make templates more useful)

## FAQ

**Q: Should I post these templates for members to see and use?**

A: Yes! Make templates visible and reusable. Consider creating a "Templates" section or pinned post.

**Q: What if members don't use the templates?**

A: Some won't, and that's okay. Templates lower the barrier for those who want structure. Power users will freestyle.

**Q: How often should I use discussion starters as a moderator?**

A: Start with 2-3x/week until you have organic member-generated content, then scale back to 1-2x/week.

**Q: Can I modify these templates?**

A: Absolutely! These are starting points. Adapt to your community's culture, niche, and needs.

**Q: What if a template doesn't get engagement?**

A: Test it 2-3 times at different times/days. If still low engagement, revise or retire it. Not every template will work for every community.
