## Email 3: Community Guidelines (Day 3)

**Subject:** How we keep [Community Name] valuable (and fun)

**Body:**

Hey [First Name],

You've probably noticed that [Community Name] feels different from other online communities. That's intentional.

We've built something special here, and it works because everyone contributes to keeping it valuable.

**Our Community Values:**

**1. Generosity First**

Share what you know. Help where you can. The rising tide lifts all boats.

**2. Real Over Perfect**

We value authentic experience over polished advice. Share your struggles, not just your wins.

**3. Signal > Noise**

Before posting, ask: "Does this add value?" We're optimizing for quality, not quantity.

**4. Support, Don't Sell**

Helpful recommendations are welcome. Pitches and spam are not. When in doubt, ask before promoting.

**5. Assume Good Intent**

We're all learning. Be kind, be patient, be constructive.

**What This Means:**

- Ask questions freely - there are no "dumb" questions here
- Share your knowledge generously
- Engage with others' posts - a comment can make someone's day
- Report anything that feels off - we take this seriously

That's it. No 47-page rulebook. Just mutual respect and genuine value.

Ready to contribute? We'd love to hear from you.

[Your Name]

**A/B Testing Ideas:**

- Subject lines (curiosity vs. clarity)
- CTA button text
- Email length (concise vs. detailed)
- Personal stories vs. community stats

**Success Metrics to Track:**

- Open rates by email
- Click-through rates on CTAs
- Profile completion rates
- First post timing (days after join)
- Response rate to engagement prompts
