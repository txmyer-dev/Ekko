- He suggests using [Mailbox.org](https://app.getrecall.ai/item/7a2198f7-2218-4095-bd01-236805666caf) or [Fastmail.com](https://app.getrecall.ai/item/0cda17f8-6d1a-4006-8d4a-6ee516ebec35) as alternatives to [Gmail](https://app.getrecall.ai/item/661f65fb-4d43-4636-85fe-a9146ff78df7), and potentially hosting your own email server for more control.
