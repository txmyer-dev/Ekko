1. BUSINESS MODEL & POSITIONING (ISC #1, #7)

Company Name (Suggested): AI Bridge Labs

Tagline: "From Excel to AI - Practical AI for Non-Technical Professionals"

First Principles Insight: This isn't an "education business" - it's a capability transfer business solving economic displacement anxiety.

Market Positioning (from Council + FirstPrinciples):

- Target: Non-technical knowledge workers (lawyers, marketers, consultants, analysts, managers) at mid-market companies (50-500 employees)
- Problem: AI displacement fear + productivity ceiling + tool overwhelm
- Solution: Hands-on AI workflow implementation (not theory)
- Differentiation: "We implement with you, then train you" vs "watch videos and figure it out"
- Market Gap: The "Excel-to-AI bridge" ($3-5B segment) - nobody targets Office power users specifically

TIER 1: AI Implementation Consulting (Launch immediately - Month 0)

- What: Done-with-you AI workflow implementation

1. STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURES (ISC #3)

SOP 1: Client Onboarding (Consulting)

1. Discovery call (30 min) - qualify fit, understand pain points
2. Proposal delivery (24-hour turnaround) - scope, timeline, pricing
3. Contract & payment (50% upfront, 50% on delivery)
4. Kickoff meeting (1 hour) - stakeholder introductions, workflow audit scheduling
5. Workflow audit (2-3 days) - shadow 3-5 team members, document current state
6. Implementation plan (1 day) - prioritize workflows, select tools, design templates
7. Implementation & training (1-2 weeks) - build workflows, train team, iterate
8. Handoff & support period (30 days) - documentation, async support
9. Case study creation (request testimonial, document results)

SOP 2: Content Creation Workflow (Courses)

1. Extract patterns from consulting engagements (what problems repeat?)
2. Define learning outcome (what can student do after this module?)
3. Build project-based exercise (real-world scenario)
4. Record instructional content (video + written guide)
5. Create assessment/deliverable (portfolio-ready output)
6. Test with beta students (iterate based on feedback)
7. Publish & promote

SOP 3: Cohort Delivery (Training Programs)

1. Launch registration (2 weeks before start, limited to 30 students)
2. Pre-cohort prep (welcome email, tool setup guide, community invite)
3. Weekly live sessions (90 min) - teach concept, demo, assign project
4. Async support (daily Slack check-ins, peer review, office hours)
5. Project submissions (students submit work, receive feedback)
6. Capstone week (final project presentations, certification)
7. Post-cohort follow-up (survey, testimonials, upsell to premium)

SOP 4: Customer Support

- Response Time: 24 hours for consulting clients, 48 hours for course students
- Channels: Email (primary), Slack (cohort students), Calendly for 1:1 calls
- Escalation: Eko handles tier-1 (FAQs, resources), Tony handles tier-2 (technical Q&A)

SOP 5: Quality Assurance

- Consulting: Every engagement produces reusable template + case study
- Courses: NPS survey after every cohort, track completion rates (target: >70%)
- Platform: Monthly usage analytics review, A/B test onboarding flows
