## Discovery Questions

*Your consultant will walk through these with you. Don't overthink — first answers are usually best.*

**1. If money weren't an issue, what would you spend your days doing?**


**2. What problem in the world frustrates you enough that you'd work on it for free?**


**3. Who do you want to help, and what does their life look like after you've helped them?**


**4. What are you doing when you feel most alive and useful?**


**5. If you could only be known for one thing, what would it be?**
